Biography

Giulia Santoro is a Palermo-based theatre actress and drama teacher with over fifteen years of experience in contemporary and classical theatre. Known for her grounded presence and emotional intensity, she builds characters that feel raw, intimate, and deeply human.

Born in Sicily and trained in Rome, Giulia developed her artistic identity between stage performance and education. Alongside her acting career, she teaches theatre and expressive movement, working with young adults and professionals who seek a deeper connection between body, voice, and emotion.

Her work often explores themes of identity, memory, and the feminine experience, blending classical texts with modern interpretations. She currently lives and works in Palermo, collaborating with independent theatre companies across Italy.
